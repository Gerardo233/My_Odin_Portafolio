Bang Bang Souce

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (e.g., Sriracha)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

How to do?

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, sweet chili sauce, hot sauce, brown sugar, and fresh lemon juice.
  2. Stir all the ingredients well until fully combined, and the sauce has a smooth and uniform consistency.
  3. Taste the sauce and adjust the flavors according to your preference. If you want it spicier, add more hot sauce. If you prefer it sweeter, add a bit more brown sugar.
  4. Once the sauce has the desired flavor, transfer it to a jar or airtight container, and store it in the refrigerator. Allow the flavors to blend for at least 30 minutes before serving to enhance the taste.
