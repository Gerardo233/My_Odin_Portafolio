Double Decker Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Hard taco shells
  • Soft flour tortillas
  • Ground beef or preferred protein
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa or hot sauce (optional)

How to do?

  1. Cook the ground beef with taco seasoning until fully cooked and seasoned.
  2. Warm the hard taco shells and set aside.
  3. Take a soft flour tortilla, spread a thin layer of refried beans (optional), and place a hard taco shell in the center.
  4. Fill the hard taco shell with the cooked ground beef and desired toppings.
  5. Fold the soft tortilla around the hard shell and filling to create the double decker taco.
  6. Repeat the process for the remaining tacos.
  7. Serve and enjoy immediately with your favorite sides or toppings.
