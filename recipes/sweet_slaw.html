Sweet Restaurant Slaw

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups shredded cabbage (green or a mix of green and purple cabbage)
  • 1 large carrot, grated
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar (or white vinegar)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to do?

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage and grated carrot.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, honey (or sugar), and Dijon mustard (if using) until well combined. The honey/sugar helps balance the acidity and provides the sweetness to the coleslaw.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage and carrot mixture. Toss everything together until the cabbage and carrot are evenly coated with the dressing.
  4. Season the coleslaw with salt and pepper to taste. You can start with a pinch of salt and a few grinds of black pepper, and then adjust according to your preference.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the coleslaw for at least 30 minutes before serving. Chilling allows the flavors to meld together and enhances the taste.
  6. Serve the Sweet Restaurant-Style Coleslaw as a refreshing side dish with burgers, sandwiches, barbecue, fried chicken, or any other main course. Enjoy!
